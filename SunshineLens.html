But that was what she used to think.

Somewhere along the line, an odd change seemed to occur within the shy girl, and somehow she ended up directly parallel to a dimension beyond her own.

The portal to this dimension was one you could not avoid, if you could call a young high school girl a “portal”.

Yes, the walking ball of sunshine, Kita Ikuyo, had become Hitori’s friend, due to circumstances she still has trouble believing.  

There are many ways you could describe the sparkling girl named Kita, but Hitori, the natural songwriter, had a few words in mind that she would never tell anyone.

But I'll tell you in her place.

She always helps everyone when it’s necessary, and doesn’t judge a person by any parameters. She was inclusive, understanding. When Hitori felt uncomfortable with a certain task or action, Kita wouldn’t force it. When Hitori needed comfort in a sad situation, Kita was always there to make her feel happy again (note: Hitori’s definition of “happy” is not the same as everyone else’s).

However, there was another aspect to Kita that Hitori felt embarrassed about. See, Hitori knows how Kita’s aura can draw people in and create happiness from nowhere. AAAAAAHHHHHH!!!!!!

Because Kita always took photos of herself, and she never failed to look pretty.
